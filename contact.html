FAQs

How do I care for my jewelry?

To ensure the longevity and beauty of your jewelry, follow these care instructions:

  1. Store your jewelry in a clean, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  2. Avoid exposing your jewelry to harsh chemicals, perfumes, and cosmetics.
  3. Remove your jewelry before swimming, showering, or engaging in activities that may cause damage.
  4. Clean your jewelry regularly using a soft brush and mild soapy water.
  5. Have your jewelry professionally inspected and cleaned periodically.

How do I measure ring size?

Measuring your ring size accurately is crucial for a comfortable fit. Follow these steps:

  1. Use a piece of string or a flexible measuring tape.
  2. Wrap the string or tape around the base of the finger where the ring will be worn.
  3. Mark the point where the string or tape meets and measure the length in millimeters.
  4. Use a ring size chart to determine the corresponding size based on the measurement.
  5. Consider the fit and style of the ring for personal comfort.

How can I make returns or exchanges?

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. Follow these steps for returns or exchanges:

  1. Contact our customer service within the specified return/exchange period.
  2. Provide the order number, reason for return/exchange, and details of the item.
  3. Wait for instructions on how to return the item or receive a replacement.
  4. Ensure the item is in its original packaging with all tags and labels intact.
  5. Once received, we will process your return or send out the replacement promptly.
